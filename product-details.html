Product Description
Key Features:
  • • CFL 1522 Cauliflower Seeds has a flexible sowing window.
  • • CFL 1522 Cauliflower has semi erect with medium plant, featuring blue green leaves.
  • • CFL 1522 Cauliflower has moderate to good temperature tolerance.
  • • It has moderate to good tolerance to Xanthomonas campestris pv. campestris (Xcc).
CFL 1522 Cauliflower Seeds Characteristics:
  • Fruit Color: Cream white
  • Fruit Shape: Compact, dome curd
  • Average curd weight: 500 gm - 850 gm
  • Average Yield:
    • Tropical: 12-13 MT /acre
    • Sub-tropical: 14-15 MT /acre
    • Temperate: 16-18 MT /acre
Sowing Details:
Season States
KharifAP, AS, BR, CT, DL, GJ, HR, JH, KA, MP, MH, OR, PB, RJ, WB, TR
SummerMH, HR, PB
  • Seed Rate: 100 - 120 gm per acre.
  • Time of Transplanting: After 21 days, seedling gets ready for transplant.
  • Spacing: Tropical: 60 x 30 cm, Sub-tropical: 60 x 45 cm, Temperate: 60 x 45 cm
  • First Harvest:
    • Tropical: 55–65 days
    • Sub-tropical: 60–75 days
    • Temperate: 75–85 days
Additional Information:
  • • CFL 1522 Cauliflower Seeds needs a balanced and adequate fertilizer supply.
  • • Cauliflower needs optimum irrigation at all stages.
  • • Use light irrigation in winter and rainy season.
  • • Use insecticides to control DBM and caterpillars in summer.
